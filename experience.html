My Employment Experience



Courtesy Clerk | Smith's Grocery Store

As a courtesy clerk at Smith's, my primary responsibilities included gathering shopping carts from corrals and bringing them back into the store for customer use. I also assisted in stocking unpurchased inventory to keep the store shelves organized and well-stocked. Bagging groceries efficiently and carefully was another key part of my role, helping customers with their checkout process. Additionally, I helped customers locate items throughout the store, ensuring they had a positive shopping experience.


Electrician Apprentice | Hedgehog Electric and Solar

As an electrician apprentice working on the construction of a store, my responsibilities included moving inventory with a pallet jack to ensure materials were readily available. I operated a scissor lift to install LED tube lights and wire store lights, including both primary and emergency circuits. My tasks also involved pulling wire through conduit and mapping where lights needed to be installed. These responsibilities allowed me to contribute to the electrical setup and functionality of the store.


MTC Carpet Cleaner | BYU Building Operations

As a carpet cleaner, my responsibilities included loading the van with necessary supplies, such as filters, the RX cleaning machine, and pre-spray solutions. I prepared the equipment by connecting water hoses and the vacuum hose to the machine in the van. After spraying carpets with cleaning solution, I operated the RX machine to thoroughly clean them. Once the cleaning was complete, I placed fans on the wet carpets to speed up the drying process.
